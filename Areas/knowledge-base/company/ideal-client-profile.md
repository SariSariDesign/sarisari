# Sari Sari Design — Ideal Client Profile

## Primary Target: Series A/B Fintech Companies

**Company stage:** Series A or Series B, recently raised capital (within the last 6 to 12 months)
**Company size:** 5 to 100 employees
**Revenue model:** B2B or B2C fintech — lending, payments, banking, insurance, wealth management, or financial infrastructure
**Design maturity:** Low to medium. They may have one designer or no dedicated design team. Design is becoming a bottleneck to shipping product.
**Key buyer:** Head of Product, VP of Product, CEO/Founder, CTO, or occasionally a first Head of Design hire who needs support scaling

## Why This Profile

Companies at this stage have three things that make them ideal:

1. They have budget. They've raised capital and have allocated spend for product development, but haven't yet committed to full-time design hires.
2. They have urgency. Post-raise, the board expects velocity. Design bottlenecks directly slow down their ability to ship and hit milestones.
3. They have complexity. Fintech products deal with compliance, trust, and regulatory requirements that generic design agencies struggle with.

## Qualification Signals

Strong fit indicators:

- Recently closed a funding round (check Crunchbase, LinkedIn announcements)
- Job postings for product designers (signals they need design capacity)
- Product involves user-facing financial transactions, onboarding, or verification
- Small or no existing design team
- Active on LinkedIn with product/design content

Weak fit or disqualifying signals:

- Pre-seed or bootstrapped with no design budget
- Large in-house design team already (10+ designers)
- Product is purely B2B API/infrastructure with no user-facing component
- Company is in deep cost-cutting mode

## Secondary Target: Adjacent Regulated Industries

We are exploring expansion into other industries with similar compliance UX needs:

- Life sciences / biotech — clinical trial interfaces, patient portals, regulatory submissions
- Investment advisory / wealth management — client portals, reporting dashboards, fiduciary compliance

The thesis is that our compliance UX expertise transfers across regulated verticals. This is being tested selectively, not pursued as a primary focus yet.

## Outreach Channels

- LinkedIn is the primary outreach channel
- Warm introductions and referrals are preferred over cold volume
- Referral partnerships with adjacent service providers (IT consulting firms, compliance consultants) are being explored
