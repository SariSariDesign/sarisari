# Sari Sari Design — Positioning

## One-Liner

Sari Sari Design is an embedded product design partner for growth-stage fintech companies.

## What We Do

We provide fractional product design capacity to Series A/B fintech companies (5–100 employees) that have recently raised capital and need senior design leadership without the overhead of full-time hires. We specialize in high-trust user flows, onboarding, compliance UX, and scalable design systems.

## Who We Serve

**Primary ICP:** Heads of Product or founding teams at Series A/B fintech companies with 5–100 employees. These companies have raised capital, are moving fast, and are hitting the point where design quality is becoming a bottleneck to growth.

**Adjacent verticals (expansion):** Regulated industries with similar UX challenges, including AI startups, life sciences, biotech, and B2B SaaS companies with compliance requirements.

## Core Differentiator

We are not a vendor, a ticket-based design shop, or a marketplace freelancer. We embed as a design partner. That means we understand your product deeply, participate in strategy conversations, and deliver work that compounds over time rather than one-off deliverables that sit in a Figma graveyard.

**Compliance UX expertise** is a genuine competitive moat. We understand SOC2, PCI DSS, KYC/AML, and how these regulatory requirements translate into user-facing design decisions. Most design agencies treat compliance as an afterthought. We treat it as a design opportunity.

## Background and Credibility

The team has shipped product design work for Meta, Disney, and Fortune 500 organizations. We bring big-company design rigor to startups that can't yet afford a full design org.

## What We Are Not

We are not an "unlimited design" subscription. We are not a logo mill. We do not compete on volume or speed of output. We compete on strategic depth, design quality, and the compounding value of an embedded relationship.
