# Sari Sari Design — Services and Pricing

## Service Model

We offer monthly retainer engagements, not project-based or hourly work. This model creates continuity, allows us to build deep product context, and produces compounding design value over time.

There is no Launch tier. We deliberately removed the entry-level tier to maintain premium positioning and signal that we are a strategic partner, not a task-completion service.

## Tier: Grow — $8,000/month

**Positioning:** Embedded design partner with foundational systems.

**What's included:**

- Dedicated senior designer (Patricia Capiral, ex-Meta)
- Ongoing UI/UX design for core product flows
- Foundational design system buildout
- High-trust flow design (onboarding, KYC, payment flows)
- Compliance UX audit and implementation (SOC2, PCI DSS, KYC/AML)
- Weekly async updates, biweekly sync calls
- Figma source files, component libraries

**Best for:** Fintech companies with an existing product that needs design consistency, better onboarding conversion, or compliance-ready interfaces before their next fundraise or audit.

## Tier: Scale — $16,000/month

**Positioning:** Fractional Head of Design with strategic business impact.

**Everything in Grow, plus:**

- Design strategy and product roadmap input
- User research and usability testing
- Cross-functional collaboration with engineering and product
- Design system governance and scaling
- Stakeholder presentations and board-ready design narratives
- Priority response and expanded availability

**Best for:** Fintech companies scaling from product-market fit to growth, where design decisions directly impact revenue, compliance posture, and fundraising optics.

## Entry Point Strategy

For new clients, we sometimes offer a small fixed-fee project (e.g., $4K logo refresh, $3K landing page audit) as a trust-building first engagement. The goal is to demonstrate quality and build the relationship, which naturally leads to a retainer conversation. This is a deliberate strategy, not a discount. We think in terms of client lifetime value, not single-project revenue.

## Pricing Signals

- We say "preferred rate" instead of "discount" when offering any concession.
- We do not publish pricing on the website. Pricing is shared in discovery calls to allow for contextualized value framing.
- The absence of a Launch tier is intentional. If a prospect's budget is below $8K/month, they are likely not our ICP.
