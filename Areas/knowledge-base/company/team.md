# Sari Sari Design — Team

## Zach — Founder

Zach is the founder of Sari Sari Design. He handles business development, client strategy, and studio operations. His background includes design work at Meta and with Fortune 500 organizations including Disney.

Zach's approach to building the studio is informed by Alex Hormozi's frameworks ($100M Leads, $100M Offers), with a focus on value-based positioning, strategic lead capture, and offer design that emphasizes risk reversal and value stacking.

He leads all client relationships, outreach, and strategic direction for the studio. He's also actively building in public — documenting the real challenges of growing a design studio, not just the wins.

## Patricia Capiral — Lead Designer

Patricia is the lead designer at Sari Sari Design. She handles the core design execution across client engagements, including product design, design systems, and high-trust UX flows.

## Studio Size

Sari Sari Design is intentionally a 2-person studio. This is a feature, not a limitation. It means clients work directly with senior-level talent on every project — there's no account manager layer, no junior handoffs, and no communication overhead. Every touchpoint is with someone who understands the full context of the engagement.
