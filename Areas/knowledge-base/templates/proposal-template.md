## Notes for the Drafter

- The proposal should read as diagnostic, not salesy. The prospect should feel like you've already started thinking about their problem.
- Lead with their pain, not your credentials. Credentials support the recommendation; they don't lead it.
- Use specific numbers and outcomes wherever possible. "$X investment" rather than "competitive pricing." "Reduce onboarding steps from 7 to 4" rather than "improve onboarding."
- Keep it to 1–2 pages. Fintech founders don't read 10-page proposals.
