# Referral Program

## Philosophy

Referrals are our highest-quality lead source. A warm introduction from a satisfied client converts faster and retains longer than any cold outreach. Our referral approach is informed by Hormozi's $100M Leads framework: make the ask easy, make the incentive compelling, and create urgency.

## Referral Incentive Structure

When asking a client for referrals, we offer a **preferred rate** (never called a "discount") for the referred company. This does two things:
1. It gives the referrer something valuable to offer their contact, making the introduction feel generous rather than transactional.
2. It creates a scarcity frame — the preferred rate is available because of the relationship, not as a standing offer.

## How to Ask

The referral ask should be:
- **Specific:** Don't say "know anyone who needs design?" Say "do you know any other fintech founders who are struggling to ship product because they don't have a design team?"
- **Easy:** Offer to draft the introduction message for them. Reduce friction to near zero.
- **Timed:** Ask after a successful delivery milestone, when satisfaction is highest. Not during onboarding, not during a rough patch.

## Active Referral Asks

- **Lendzi:** Referral request drafted using Hormozi framework. Includes preferred-rate incentive and scarcity framing. Pending deployment at the right moment in the relationship.
